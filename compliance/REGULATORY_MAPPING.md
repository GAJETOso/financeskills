## Regional / Local Frameworks

### Nigeria
Full details: [standards/national-gaap/nigeria.md](../standards/national-gaap/nigeria.md)

- **Tax authority**: Nigeria Revenue Service (NRS) — formerly FIRS; renamed under Nigeria Revenue Service (Establishment) Act 2025
- **Tax law**: Nigeria Tax Act 2025 (consolidates CITA, PITA, VAT Act, and others)
- **Listed / large entities**: IFRS (mandatory from 2012; regulated by FRCN)
- **Public sector / MDAs**: IPSAS (FGN phased adoption)
- **Oil and Gas**: IFRS + NUPRC reporting; Petroleum Industry Act 2021 (PIA) fiscal terms
- **Banking**: IFRS 9 + CBN Prudential Guidelines + Basel III (CBN implementation)
- **Governance**: SEC Nigeria Code (2021); CBN Governance Code; King IV-aligned

### Africa (OHADA zone)
- **Financial Analysis / Audit**: AUDCIF (Acte Uniforme relatif au Droit Comptable)
- Skills: audit-checklist (OHADA internal controls)

### South Africa
- IFRS for listed entities; King IV for governance; IoDSA aligned
- SARS: Income Tax Act 1962; VAT Act 1991

### Kenya
- Finance Act (updated annually; Digital Service Tax provisions)

## Implementation Rules

1. **Framework Detection**: Skills must identify the reporting framework at execution start before applying any standard-specific logic.
2. **Standards Reference**: Cite the applicable standard paragraph in output (e.g., "per IAS 1.54" or "per IPSAS 24.21").
3. **Accuracy over Automation**: Any conflict between AI-generated output and regulatory text must be flagged for manual review with a confidence level.
4. **Audit Trail**: All compliance-mapped skills must output a standard reference log as part of their result.
5. **Dual-framework conflicts**: Where IFRS and US GAAP diverge materially, surface the difference explicitly and request framework confirmation.
6. **Nigeria Tax Update**: FIRS is now NRS (Nigeria Revenue Service) under the Nigeria Tax Act 2025 — use NRS in all Nigeria tax references.
7. **Standards Library**: For detailed standard content, link agents to `../standards/README.md` as the canonical lookup source.
