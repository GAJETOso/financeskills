# Regulatory Compliance Mapping

This document maps FinanceSkills to international and local regulatory frameworks to ensure AI-driven tasks remain compliant with accounting standards.

## 🌍 International Frameworks

### IFRS (International Financial Reporting Standards)
- **Financial Analysis**: Mapped to *IAS 1 (Presentation of Financial Statements)* and *IAS 7 (Statement of Cash Flows)*.
- **Investment Analysis**: Mapped to *IFRS 9 (Financial Instruments)* and *IFRS 13 (Fair Value Measurement)*.
- **Audit Checklist**: Covers controls required by *ISA (International Standards on Auditing)*.

### US GAAP (Generally Accepted Accounting Principles)
- **Tax Planning**: Mapped to *ASC 740 (Income Taxes)*.
- **Budget Forecasting**: Aligned with *FASB* guidelines on management discussion and analysis (MD&A).
- **Financial Analysis**: Mapped to *ASC 205 (Presentation of Financial Statements)*.

## 🇿🇦 Local / Regional Frameworks (Africa focus)

### OHADA (West/Central Africa)
- **Financial Analysis**: Mapped to the *Acte Uniforme relatif au Droit Comptable et à l'Information Financière (AUDCIF)*.
- **Audit Checklist**: Covers internal controls required for OHADA-compliant entities.

### South African Revenue Service (SARS)
- **Tax Planning**: Mapped to the *Income Tax Act, 1962* and *VAT Act, 1991*.

### Kenyan Finance Act
- **Tax Planning**: Updated annually to reflect changes in the *Finance Act* (e.g., Digital Service Tax).

## 🛠️ Implementation Rules
1.  **Context Detection**: Skills must first identify the reporting framework (IFRS vs. GAAP).
2.  **Accuracy over Automation**: Any conflict between AI logic and regulatory text must be flagged for manual review.
3.  **Audit Trail**: All compliant-mapped skills must output an audit log referencing the specific standard (e.g., "Compliant with IAS 1.54").
