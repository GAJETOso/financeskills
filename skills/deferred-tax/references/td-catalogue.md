# Temporary Difference Catalogue (worked examples)

Assume 30% corporate rate (Nigeria CIT for large companies; substitute local rate).

## 1. PP&E — Capital Allowances Faster Than Depreciation
Asset cost 100M. Year 1: book depreciation 10M (carrying 90M); capital allowances 35M (tax base 65M).
TD = 90 − 65 = 25M taxable → **DTL 7.5M**. Reverses as allowances exhaust while depreciation continues.

## 2. Provision Deductible on Payment
Leave pay provision 8M booked; deductible only when paid → tax base 0.
TD = deductible 8M → **DTA 2.4M**, recognized if future profits probable.

## 3. Tax Losses
Unused losses 50M, indefinite carryforward. Forecast supports utilization of 30M over planning horizon → recognize **DTA 9M** (30M × 30%); disclose unrecognized 20M. Recent-loss history demands convincing evidence — board-approved forecasts with achievable assumptions, contracted revenues.

## 4. Revaluation Surplus
Building revalued +40M, tax base unchanged → DTL 12M **through OCI**, not P&L.

## 5. Leases (post-2021 IAS 12 amendment)
ROU 31.3M and liability 31.3M at commencement, both tax bases nil (payments deducted when paid): recognize gross DTL 9.39M (ROU) and DTA 9.39M (liability) — net nil day one, diverges as depreciation and liability unwind at different paces.

## 6. Intragroup Profit in Inventory
Sub sold goods to parent at 5M profit; still in parent inventory at year-end. Eliminate profit on consolidation; tax was PAID by the sub → **DTA = 5M × buyer's rate** (consolidation-level entry).

## 7. ECL on Loans (banks)
ECL booked 100M; tax allows specific impairments only, say 60M → deductible TD 40M → DTA 12M.

## ETR Reconciliation Skeleton
```
Profit before tax                          1,000
Tax at statutory 30%                         300
+ Non-deductible expenses (fines, ent.)       15
− Exempt income (FGN bond interest)          (24)
+ Unrecognized DTA on current-year losses     10
± Rate change remeasurement                    6
± Prior-year true-up                          (4)
= Tax expense                                303   → ETR 30.3%
```
Every line needs a workpaper. An unexplained plug means the TD schedule and the current tax computation disagree — reconcile them, don't force it.
