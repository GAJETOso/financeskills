# Retirement Planning Fundamentals

## How Much Do You Need? — The Nest Egg Target

The most widely cited rule is the **4% Safe Withdrawal Rate** (Bengen, 1994):
`Nest Egg = Desired Annual Income / 0.04`

A $1.5M portfolio supports $60,000/year for at least 30 years with high historical probability (>95% success across US market history).

**Conservative alternative**: Use 3.5% for a 40+ year horizon (FIRE movement standard).

## Employer Match — Never Leave It on the Table
An employer 4% match on a $80,000 salary = $3,200 free per year. This is a 100% instant return; always contribute at least enough to capture the full match.

## Rule of 72
A quick mental check: `Years to Double = 72 / Annual Return %`
At 7% return, money doubles every ~10.3 years.
