# Risk Metrics: VaR & Z-Score

Quantitative tools for measuring the probability and impact of financial failure.

## 🏛️ Value at Risk (VaR)
VaR measures the potential loss in value of a risky asset or portfolio over a defined period for a given confidence interval.

### Interpretation
"Our 1-day 95% VaR is $1M." 
This means there is a **5% probability** that the portfolio will lose **more than $1M** in a single day.

---

## 🔍 Altman Z-Score
A formula used to predict the probability that a firm will go into bankruptcy within two years.

### The Zones
- **Z > 2.99**: "Safe" Zone.
- **1.81 < Z < 2.99**: "Grey" Zone (Caution required).
- **Z < 1.81**: "Distress" Zone (High probability of bankruptcy).
