# Standard Close Task Library

## Pre-Close (WD-3 to WD-0)
| Task | Owner (typical) | Depends On | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Issue close calendar & cut-off memo | Controller | — | Include AP invoice cut-off datetime |
| Recurring/standing JE templates refreshed | GL accountant | — | Depreciation, amortization, allocations |
| Chase prior-month open rec items | Account owners | — | Items > 30 days escalate |
| Pre-accrue known invoices (rent, utilities run-rate) | GL accountant | — | True-up at WD2 |
| FX rates loaded (closing & average) | Treasury | Central bank/Reuters publication | Single official source |

## WD1
AP subledger close → GRNI report; AR close + billing cut-off; payroll posting; fixed asset additions/disposals processed; bank statements loaded; petty cash/cards cut-off.

## WD2
Accruals (AP unbilled, payroll burden, bonus, audit fees); prepaid amortization; depreciation run; inventory count adjustments + costing run; revenue cut-off testing; intercompany invoicing complete + initial matching report; deferred revenue release.

## WD3
Bank recs; AR/AP subledger-to-GL recs; intercompany differences cleared (tolerance rule, e.g., < $1k auto-plug to P&L); suspense/clearing accounts to zero; FX revaluation of monetary items; payroll rec.

## WD4
Entity TB lock; consolidation run + eliminations; tax provision (current + deferred-tax skill); management/late adjustments (controller approval only); goodwill/impairment indicators check (quarterly).

## WD5
Flux analysis vs. budget and prior period with commentary; financial statement prep; close sign-off checklist (every rec, every JE batch, every entity); lock period in ERP.

## Fast-Close Levers (ranked by typical impact)
1. Pre-close accruals for predictable costs (move work off critical path).
2. Intercompany dispute deadline at WD-0 with auto-plug rule.
3. Materiality-tiered recs: low-risk accounts quarterly, not monthly.
4. Standing JE automation in ERP; kill spreadsheet uploads.
5. Hard AP cut-off discipline — late invoices accrue, never reopen.
