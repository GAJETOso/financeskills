# IFRS Accounting for Digital Assets

Guidance on how to apply International Financial Reporting Standards to cryptocurrencies and other digital assets.

## 🏛️ Classification Logic

### 1. Intangible Assets (IAS 38)
The standard treatment for cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin.
- **Criteria**: Identifiable non-monetary asset without physical substance.
- **Measurement**: Cost model or Revaluation model (if an active market exists).

### 2. Inventory (IAS 2)
Applicable if the entity holds the assets for sale in the ordinary course of business (e.g., broker-dealers).
- **Measurement**: Lower of cost and net realizable value (NRV).

---

## 🔍 Key Challenges
- **Impairment**: Determining if a price drop is "prolonged" or "material."
- **Custody**: Verifying that the entity has control over the private keys.
- **Valuation**: Selecting a representative exchange price (Level 1 input).
