# Worked Example - audit-checklist

## Ask

> We are preparing for our year-end audit. Can you review our Procure-to-Pay process for potential gaps? We deal with about 50 vendors.

## What a correct response contains

Should identify key audit objectives for P2P: Existence, Completeness, and Valuation. Should focus on the 'Three-Way Match' control. Should suggest a search for unrecorded liabilities by looking at post-year-end payments. Should provide a structured list of findings with Risk ratings and Recommendations.

## File-driven variant

With fixture(s) `evals/files/control_register.csv`:

> Review the attached control register (evals/files/control_register.csv) as part of interim audit planning. Identify control gaps, segregation-of-duties conflicts, and overdue performance; classify severity and propose the audit response for each.

Expected: Findings: C-02 missing evidence for two months (operating effectiveness cannot be relied on - extend substantive testing on credit notes); C-03 preparer-approver SoD conflict on bank recs (design deficiency); C-05 user access review two quarters overdue (ITGC gap - affects reliance on automated controls incl. C-01). C-01 and C-04 satisfactory.
