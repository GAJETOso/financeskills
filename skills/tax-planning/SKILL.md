# 📑 Tax Planning Skill

## When to Use
Use this skill to optimize tax liability for corporate or individual entities by analyzing income, expenses, and current tax codes.

## Initial Assessment
- Identify the tax jurisdiction (e.g., US Federal, UK HMRC).
- Gather all sources of income and deductible expenses.
- Determine the filing entity type (Individual, LLC, Corporation).

## Steps
1.  **Data Extraction**: Parse financial statements or transaction logs to categorize taxable income and potential deductions.
2.  **Liability Calculation**: Apply current tax brackets and rules to estimate the baseline tax liability.
3.  **Strategy Identification**: Search for applicable tax credits, deferral opportunities (e.g., 401k, pension), and asset depreciation strategies.
4.  **Simulation**: Run scenarios with different strategies (e.g., "What if we maximize 179 depreciation?") to find the lowest legal tax path.
5.  **Audit Trail**: Generate a report documenting every deduction claimed and the reasoning behind it.

## Output Format
- **Summary**: Total estimated liability and potential savings.
- **Detailed Breakdown**: Categorized list of income, deductions, and credits.
- **Action Plan**: Priority list of steps to implement the strategy.

## ⚖️ Disclaimer
AI-generated tax planning is for analysis and automation only. Always have a certified tax professional or CPA review the final plan before filing.
