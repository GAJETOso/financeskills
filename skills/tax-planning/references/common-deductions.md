# Common Deductions: Business Expense Guide

A checklist of standard eligible expenses that can lower a business's taxable income.

## 🏛️ Standard Categories

### 1. Operations
- **Rent/Lease**: Office space or equipment rentals.
- **Utilities**: Electricity, water, internet, and phone.
- **Supplies**: Office consumables, software subscriptions (SaaS).

### 2. Marketing & Travel
- **Advertising**: Online ads, agency fees, print materials.
- **Travel**: 100% of transport; percentage of meals (varies by year/jurisdiction).

### 3. Personnel
- **Salaries**: W-2 wages and employer-side payroll taxes.
- **Benefits**: Health insurance premiums, retirement contributions.

---

## 🔍 Special Deductions
- **Section 179 (US)**: Immediate expensing of the full purchase price of qualifying equipment.
- **R&D Credits**: Dollar-for-dollar tax reductions for innovative activities.
