# Tax Code Lookup: Global Sources

Directories and portals for accessing the most up-to-date tax laws and regulations.

## 🏛️ Major Portals

### United States
- **IRS (Internal Revenue Service)**: [Official Website](https://www.irs.gov/). Use the "Search" function for specific Publication numbers (e.g., Pub 535 for Business Expenses).

### South Africa
- **SARS (South African Revenue Service)**: [Official Website](https://www.sars.gov.za/). Key link: [Legal Counsel / Primary Legislation](https://www.sars.gov.za/legal-counsel/primary-legislation/).

### United Kingdom
- **HMRC (HM Revenue & Customs)**: [Official Manuals](https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/hm-revenue-customs/about/publications).

---

## 🔍 Best Practices for Searching
1.  **Check the Year**: Tax laws change annually. Ensure you are looking at the code for the correct filing year.
2.  **Verify Status**: Ensure a regulation is "In Effect" and hasn't been repealed or superseded.
3.  **Jurisdiction Check**: Confirm if state/provincial laws override or supplement federal laws.
