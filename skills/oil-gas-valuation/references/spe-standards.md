# SPE Petroleum Resources Management System (PRMS)

The industry-standard framework for classifying and estimating petroleum resources.

## 🏛️ Classification Categories

### 1. Proved Reserves (1P)
- **P90**: There is at least a 90% probability that the quantities actually recovered will equal or exceed the estimate.
- **Criteria**: Based on geological and engineering data; must be economically producible under existing economic conditions.

### 2. Probable Reserves (2P)
- **P50**: The sum of Proved and Probable reserves where there is at least a 50% probability that the quantities actually recovered will equal or exceed the estimate.

### 3. Possible Reserves (3P)
- **P10**: The sum of Proved, Probable, and Possible reserves where there is at least a 10% probability that the quantities actually recovered will equal or exceed the estimate.

---

## 🔍 Key Definitions
- **BOE (Barrel of Oil Equivalent)**: Used to combine oil and gas into a single measure based on energy content (typically 6 Mcf of gas = 1 Bbl of oil).
- **RLI (Reserve Life Index)**: Total Reserves / Annual Production.
