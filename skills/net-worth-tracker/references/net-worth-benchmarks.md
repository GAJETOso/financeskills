# Net Worth Benchmarks

## The Millionaire Next Door Formula
From Stanley & Danko (1996), a rule of thumb for expected net worth at a given age:

`Expected Net Worth = Age × Annual Pre-Tax Income / 10`

- **Prodigious Accumulator of Wealth (PAW)**: Net worth > 2× expected.
- **Average Accumulator of Wealth (AAW)**: Net worth ≈ expected.
- **Under Accumulator of Wealth (UAW)**: Net worth < 0.5× expected.

## Healthy Ratio Targets

| Ratio | Healthy Range |
|---|---|
| Debt-to-Asset Ratio | < 50% (ideally < 30%) |
| Liquid Asset Ratio | ≥ 10% of total assets |
| Investment Asset Ratio | ≥ 40% of total assets (by mid-career) |
| Emergency Fund | 3–6 months of expenses in liquid form |
