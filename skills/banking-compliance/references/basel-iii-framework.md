# Basel III: Regulatory Capital Framework

The global regulatory framework for bank capital adequacy, stress testing, and market liquidity risk.

## 🏛️ The Three Pillars

### Pillar 1: Minimum Capital Requirements
- **CET1 Ratio**: Minimum 4.5% of Risk-Weighted Assets (RWA).
- **Tier 1 Ratio**: Minimum 6.0% of RWA.
- **Total Capital Ratio**: Minimum 8.0% of RWA.

### Pillar 2: Supervisory Review
Ensures that banks have adequate internal processes to assess their capital adequacy and that supervisors review these assessments.

### Pillar 3: Market Discipline
Mandatory disclosure requirements to provide market participants with information about a bank's risk profile and capital adequacy.

---

## 🔍 Key Buffers
- **Capital Conservation Buffer (CCB)**: An additional 2.5% of CET1 to withstand periods of stress.
- **Countercyclical Buffer (CCyB)**: Applied during periods of high credit growth to protect the banking sector.
