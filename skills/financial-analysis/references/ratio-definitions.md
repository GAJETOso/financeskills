# Financial Ratio Definitions

### Liquidity Ratios
- **Current Ratio**: Current Assets / Current Liabilities. Measures ability to pay short-term obligations.
- **Quick Ratio**: (Current Assets - Inventory) / Current Liabilities. A more stringent liquidity test.

### Profitability Ratios
- **Gross Margin**: (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue. Percentage of revenue exceeding cost of goods sold.
- **Net Margin**: Net Income / Revenue. The "bottom line" profitability.
- **ROE (Return on Equity)**: Net Income / Shareholders' Equity. How effectively management uses equity to generate profit.

### Solvency Ratios
- **Debt-to-Equity**: Total Liabilities / Shareholders' Equity. The company's financial leverage.
- **Interest Coverage**: EBIT / Interest Expense. Ability to pay interest on outstanding debt.
