# IAA: International Actuarial Standards of Practice

## What It Addresses
The IAA develops model actuarial standards and educational notes. Most jurisdictions adopt national actuarial standards based on IAA model standards. Key actuarial organisations include:

| Region | Body | Standards |
|--------|------|-----------|
| Global | IAA | ISAPs (model standards) |
| UK/Ireland | IFoA (Institute and Faculty of Actuaries) | Technical Actuarial Standards (TAS) |
| US | AAA / SOA / CAS | ASOPs (Actuarial Standards of Practice) |
| South Africa | ASSA | APN (Actuarial Practice Notes) |
| Nigeria | NIA (Nigerian Institute of Actuaries) | Adopts IAA/IFoA standards |

## ISAP Series (International Standards of Actuarial Practice)

| Standard | Title | Key Use |
|----------|-------|---------|
| ISAP 1 | General Actuarial Practice | Foundational principles; scope; communication |
| ISAP 1A | Governance of Models | Model validation; documentation; change control |
| ISAP 2 | Social Security Programs | Government pension and social insurance valuations |
| ISAP 3 | Actuarial Practice Under IFRS | IFRS-related financial reporting actuarial work |
| ISAP 4 | IFRS 17 Insurance Contracts | Actuarial inputs to IFRS 17 GMM/PAA/VFA models |
| ISAP 5 | General Insurance Reserving | P&C reserve estimation |
| ISAP 6 | Enterprise Risk Management | ERM actuarial framework |

## When Actuarial Input Is Required in Financial Reporting
- **IAS 19 / IPSAS 39**: Defined benefit pension obligations — actuarial valuation mandatory; key assumptions (discount rate, mortality, salary growth, turnover) drive DBO and service cost
- **IFRS 17**: CSM; risk adjustment; loss component — actuarial models central
- **IAS 37 / IPSAS 19**: Long-tail provisions (insurance guarantees, nuclear decommissioning, asbestos) — actuarial projection methods
- **Solvency II / NAICOM**: Insurance technical provisions — best estimate liability + risk margin
