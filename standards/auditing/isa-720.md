# ISA 720: Other Information

## What It Addresses
Addresses the auditor's responsibilities with respect to other information in documents containing audited FS (e.g., annual report, integrated report). The auditor must read and consider other information for material inconsistencies with the FS or material misstatements of fact.

## Key Requirements
- **Read other information**: Identify material inconsistencies with audited FS or the auditor's knowledge
- **If material inconsistency**: Determine whether the FS or other information requires revision; communicate to management/TCWG
- **Report**: If material inconsistency unresolved, describe in the auditor's report
- **Scope**: Annual reports; integrated reports; sustainability reports appended to financial statements

## Sustainability / ESG Context
As IFRS S1/S2 sustainability disclosures are increasingly attached to financial statements or annual reports, ISA 720 is relevant for auditors considering consistency of sustainability disclosures with audited financial information. ISAE 3000/3410 addresses assurance specifically on sustainability information.
