# ISA 700 Series: Audit Opinions and Reporting

## ISA 700: Forming and Expressing an Opinion
- Requires evaluation of: whether FS are prepared in accordance with applicable framework; presentation is fair; disclosures are adequate
- **Opinion types**: Unmodified ("true and fair view") — FS fairly presented in all material respects
- **Report structure**: Title; Addressee; Auditor's Opinion; Basis for Opinion; KAMs (if listed); Other Reporting Responsibilities; Signature; Date; Location

## ISA 701: Key Audit Matters (Listed Entities)
- **Scope**: Mandatory for listed entities; voluntary for others
- **What to include**: Matters of greatest significance in the audit — highest assessed risks, significant judgements on estimates, significant events or transactions
- **Format per matter**: Description of KAM; how the matter was addressed in the audit
- **Excluded from KAM**: Material uncertainty related to going concern (separate section per ISA 570); matters not communicated to TCWG

## ISA 705: Modifications to the Opinion
| Situation | Nature of Modification | Type of Opinion |
|-----------|----------------------|----------------|
| Material but not pervasive misstatement | Qualified | "except for" |
| Material and pervasive misstatement | Adverse | "do not give a true and fair view" |
| Material but not pervasive limitation on scope | Qualified | "except for" |
| Material and pervasive limitation on scope | Disclaimer | No opinion expressed |

## ISA 706: Emphasis of Matter and Other Matter Paragraphs
- **Emphasis of Matter**: Draw attention to a matter properly presented/disclosed but fundamental to users' understanding (e.g., going concern; significant uncertainty; restatement)
- **Other Matter**: Draw attention to a matter relevant to users' understanding of the audit, the auditor's responsibilities, or the auditor's report

## ISA 800/805/810: Special Purpose
- ISA 800: Audits of FS prepared under special purpose frameworks (cash basis, regulatory, contractual)
- ISA 805: Single FS or specific elements, accounts, or items
- ISA 810: Summary FS — express opinion on whether summary FS are consistent with audited FS
