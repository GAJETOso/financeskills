# ISA 800/805/810: Special Purpose Audits

## ISA 800: Audits Under Special Purpose Frameworks
- Applies when FS are prepared under a framework other than IFRS or national GAAP (e.g., cash basis, modified cash basis, regulatory basis, contractual basis)
- Auditor must assess whether the framework is acceptable given the purpose; include Emphasis of Matter paragraph restricting distribution/use
- **IPSAS context**: IPSAS is a special purpose framework for government entities — ISA 800 considerations apply for governments transitioning to or reporting under IPSAS where a full fair presentation framework hasn't been adopted

## ISA 805: Single FS / Specific Elements
- Engagement to audit a single FS (e.g., balance sheet only) or a specific account balance (e.g., debtors) or type of transaction
- Must comply with all ISAs adaptable to the engagement

## ISA 810: Summary FS
- Engagement to report on whether summary FS are consistent with or derived from the complete audited FS
- Must determine whether the summarisation criteria are reasonable
- Opinion on summary FS does not substitute for the complete FS
