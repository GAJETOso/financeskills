# ISA Overview: International Standards on Auditing

## What They Address
The ISAs set out the requirements and guidance for independent auditors conducting audits of historical financial information. They apply globally and are adopted (sometimes with national modifications) by most jurisdictions including Nigeria (FRCN), UK (FRC), EU, and others.

## Structure by Series

### 100–199: Introductory
- ISA 200: Overall objectives; reasonable assurance; professional scepticism

### 200–299: General Principles and Responsibilities
- ISA 210: Agreeing terms of engagement
- ISA 220: Quality control for an audit (superseded by ISA 220 Revised 2022)
- ISA 230: Audit documentation
- ISA 240: Fraud responsibilities
- ISA 250: Laws and regulations
- ISA 260: Communication with TCWG
- ISA 265: Communicating deficiencies in internal control

### 300–499: Risk Assessment and Response
- ISA 300: Planning an audit
- ISA 315 (Revised 2019): Risk identification and assessment — updated for IT general controls, complexity
- ISA 320: Materiality
- ISA 330: Responses to assessed risks
- ISA 402: Service organisations (as user auditor)
- ISA 450: Evaluation of misstatements

### 500–599: Audit Evidence
- ISA 500: Audit evidence generally
- ISA 501: Specific items — inventory, litigation, segment
- ISA 505: External confirmations
- ISA 510: Opening balances
- ISA 520: Analytical procedures
- ISA 530: Audit sampling
- ISA 540 (Revised 2019): Accounting estimates — updated spectrum of subjectivity
- ISA 550: Related parties
- ISA 560: Subsequent events
- ISA 570 (Revised): Going concern — enhanced reporting requirements
- ISA 580: Written representations

### 600–699: Using the Work of Others
- ISA 600 (Revised 2022): Group audits — major revision; lead auditor responsibility
- ISA 610: Internal auditors
- ISA 620: Auditor's expert

### 700–799: Audit Conclusions and Reporting
- ISA 700: Forming and expressing an opinion
- ISA 701: Key Audit Matters (KAMs) — listed entities
- ISA 705: Modifications to the opinion
- ISA 706: Emphasis of Matter and Other Matter paragraphs
- ISA 710: Comparative information
- ISA 720: Other information in documents containing audited financial statements

### 800–899: Specialised Areas
- ISA 800: Audits of special purpose frameworks (cash basis, regulatory basis)
- ISA 805: Single financial statements / specific elements
- ISA 810: Engagements to report on summary financial statements

## Key Principles Across All ISAs
- **Professional scepticism**: Questioning mind; critical assessment of audit evidence
- **Professional judgement**: Application of relevant knowledge, experience, and standards
- **Reasonable assurance**: Not absolute; material misstatement detected with high (not absolute) confidence
- **Audit risk model:** AR = IR × CR × DR — inherent risk × control risk × detection risk

## Nigeria Context
- FRCN mandates ISAs for statutory audits in Nigeria
- ICAN and ANAN members are required to comply with ISAs
- Group audit considerations (ISA 600) relevant for multinational oil & gas JVs and banking groups
