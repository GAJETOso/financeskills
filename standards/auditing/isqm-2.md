# ISQM 2: Engagement Quality Reviews (EQR)

## What It Addresses
Establishes requirements for engagement quality reviews (EQR) — an objective evaluation of significant judgements made by the engagement team and conclusions reached.

## Scope
- **Mandatory EQR**: All listed entity audits; public interest entity audits; engagements with specific circumstances identified by firm risk assessment
- **Voluntary EQR**: Firm may designate additional engagements based on quality risk assessment

## Key Requirements
- **Independence**: EQR reviewer must be independent of the engagement; cannot be a member of the engagement team
- **Timing**: EQR completed before the auditor's report is dated
- **Scope of review**: Significant judgements and conclusions on: significant risks; modifications or emphasis; going concern; KAMs (ISA 701); group audit considerations
- **Documentation**: Procedures performed; basis for conclusions; EQR reviewer's conclusion that no significant matters remain unresolved
- **Consultation**: Engagement team must consult with EQR reviewer on significant matters as they arise during the audit
