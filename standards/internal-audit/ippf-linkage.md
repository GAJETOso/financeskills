# IIA GIAS ↔ Other Framework Linkage

## ISA 610 — External Auditor Use of Internal Audit Work
External auditors (ISA 610) can use internal audit work after evaluating:
- Objectivity of the internal audit function (GIAS Domain II / III)
- Competence of internal auditors
- Systematic and disciplined approach (GIAS Domain V)
Where reliance is placed, external auditor still responsible for conclusions.

## COSO Frameworks
GIAS Domain V assurance engagements frequently assess controls designed using the COSO Internal Control — Integrated Framework. COSO ERM (2017) is the primary enterprise risk framework against which internal audit risk assessments are benchmarked.

## SOX 404 (US Listed Entities / SEC-registered)
Internal audit typically leads management's assessment of ICFR under SOX 404(a). External auditors then attest under SOX 404(b). PCAOB AS 2201 governs external auditor ICFR attestation. GIAS Domain V directly supports this process.

## Sarbanes-Oxley and Nigerian Equivalents
- SEC Nigeria's Code of Corporate Governance (2011, revised) references internal audit effectiveness
- CBN Code of Corporate Governance for Banks references internal audit independence and reporting lines consistent with GIAS
