# FRS-101: Reduced Disclosure Framework

## What It Addresses
Permits qualifying entities (subsidiaries of groups applying IFRS) to apply UK-adopted IFRS recognition and measurement but with significant disclosure reductions.

## Scope
UK/Irish subsidiaries whose parent prepares publicly available consolidated financial statements under IFRS and all shareholders agree to reduced disclosures.

## Key Requirements
- Apply same recognition and measurement as UK-adopted IFRS (full IFRS amounts)
- Exempt from many IFRS disclosures (cash flow statement, segment information, management of capital, fair value hierarchy, share-based payment details, first-time adoption)
- Must still comply with Companies Act minimum disclosures

## Key Disclosures
- Disclosures exempted and name of parent producing consolidated IFRS statements

## Relationship to IFRS
Allows subsidiary to report identical numbers to IFRS parent with significantly fewer disclosures. No equivalent in IFRS framework (IFRS 19 is a forthcoming analogue).
