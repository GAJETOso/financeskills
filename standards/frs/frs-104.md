# FRS-104: Interim Financial Reporting

## What It Addresses
Prescribes the content of an interim financial report for entities applying FRS 102.

## Scope
Entities applying FRS 102 that prepare interim financial reports.

## Key Requirements
- Same accounting policies as annual FRS 102 statements
- Condensed interim statements permitted
- Disclosure: significant events and transactions since last annual report
- Estimated annual effective tax rate applied to interim period income

## Key Disclosures
- Statement that same policies as annual statements
- Seasonal nature of operations; unusual items
- Changes in estimates since last annual report

## Relationship to IFRS
Adapted from IAS 34. Less prescriptive than IAS 34 for FRS 102 entities.
