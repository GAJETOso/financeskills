# FRS-100: Application of Financial Reporting Requirements

## What It Addresses
Sets out the financial reporting framework applicable in the UK and Republic of Ireland; determines which standard applies to each type of entity.

## Scope
All entities required to prepare statutory financial statements under UK/Irish law.

## Key Requirements
- Listed entities and their subsidiaries: must use UK-adopted IFRS (or EU-adopted for Irish entities)
- Large/medium unlisted companies: use FRS 102
- Small companies: may use FRS 102 or FRS 105 (if micro-entity)
- Micro-entities: may use FRS 105
- Charities: use FRS 102 + Charities SORP
- Public sector: typically FRS 102 or IPSAS

## Key Disclosures
- Which framework applied; reason if moving between frameworks

## Relationship to IFRS
Gateway standard — no private sector equivalent outside UK/Irish framework.
