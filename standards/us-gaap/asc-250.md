# ASC-250: Accounting Changes and Error Corrections

## What It Addresses
Accounting for and disclosure of accounting changes (changes in accounting principle, estimates, and reporting entity) and correction of errors.

## Key Requirements
- Change in accounting principle: retrospective application to all prior periods presented (unless impracticable)
- Change in accounting estimate: prospective treatment; no restatement
- Change in reporting entity: retrospective restatement of all periods
- Correction of errors: restatement of prior period financial statements (reissuance if material)

## Key Disclosures
- Nature and reason for change
- Effect on line items and per-share amounts
- Cumulative effect of change on retained earnings
- For error corrections: nature, effect on prior period income, and EPS

## Key Differences from IFRS
IAS 8 is the IFRS equivalent. Substantially converged. Key difference: IFRS prohibits LIFO; change from LIFO to another method under US GAAP requires retrospective application.
