# ASC-330: Inventory

## What It Addresses
Measurement, recognition, and disclosure of inventory; lower of cost and net realisable value (LCNRV); cost formulas.

## Key Requirements
- FIFO, weighted average, or LIFO permitted (LIFO prohibited under IFRS)
- Measure at LCNRV (lower of cost and net realisable value) for most entities; LIFO/retail method entities use lower of cost or market (LCM)
- LCNRV: NRV = estimated selling price less reasonably predictable costs of completion, disposal, and transportation
- Write-down to LCNRV is permanent — no reversal permitted (contrast with IFRS 2 which allows reversal)
- Abnormal amounts of idle facility expense, freight, and handling costs: charge to period

## Key Disclosures
- Accounting policy for cost formula
- Basis for stating inventories (cost, LCNRV)
- Inventory by classification (raw materials, WIP, finished goods)
- LIFO reserve and LIFO liquidation effects (if LIFO used)

## Key Differences from IFRS
IAS 2 prohibits LIFO; permits reversal of NRV write-downs; no LIFO reserve concept. US GAAP write-downs are permanent; IAS 2 allows reversal.
