# ASC-230: Statement of Cash Flows

## What It Addresses
Classification of cash receipts and payments as operating, investing, or financing activities; reporting requirements for the statement of cash flows.

## Key Requirements
- Three sections: operating (direct or indirect method), investing, and financing
- Interest paid and received: operating; dividends received: operating; dividends paid: financing or operating (policy choice)
- Non-cash investing and financing activities: disclosed separately
- Restricted cash: included in cash and cash equivalents on the statement; reconciliation required
- ASU 2016-18: restricted cash included in beginning/ending balances

## Key Disclosures
- Restricted cash amounts
- Non-cash transactions
- Policy for classifying interest and dividends

## Key Differences from IFRS
IAS 7 is the IFRS equivalent. IFRS allows more classification flexibility for interest and dividends. IFRS requires interest paid as financing if capitalised to assets.
