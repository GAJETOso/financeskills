# ASC-450: Contingencies

## What It Addresses
Accounting for and disclosure of loss contingencies and gain contingencies.

## Key Requirements
- Loss contingency: accrue when (1) probable that liability has been incurred and (2) amount can be reasonably estimated
- Probable = likely to occur
- Measurement: best estimate; if range and no amount better than another, accrue minimum of range
- Disclosure: if reasonably possible (not remote) but not accrued, disclose nature and range of possible loss
- Gain contingencies: generally not recognised; disclose when probable but avoid misleading implication of realisation

## Key Disclosures
- Nature of contingency
- Estimate of possible loss or range; or statement that estimate cannot be made
- For accrued contingencies: nature and amount

## Key Differences from IFRS
IAS 37: recognise when 'more likely than not' (>50%) obligation exists vs. US GAAP 'probable'. Constructive obligations more explicitly addressed in IFRS. IFRS requires discounting if material.
