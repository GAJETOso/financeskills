# ASC-850: Related Party Disclosures

## What It Addresses
Disclosure requirements for transactions and balances with related parties including parent companies, subsidiaries, affiliates, and key management.

## Key Requirements
- Disclose material related party transactions: nature of relationship, description of transaction, dollar amounts, amounts due to/from related parties
- Relationships requiring disclosure: parent/subsidiaries; affiliates; entities under common control; individuals/entities that can exercise significant influence; principal owners; management; immediate family
- Transactions in the ordinary course of business at market rates: may disclose or not (judgement)
- Representations that transactions were conducted at arm's length: permitted only when supportable

## Key Disclosures
- Nature of relationships; description of transactions
- Dollar amounts of transactions; amounts outstanding
- Effect on financial statements

## Key Differences from IFRS
IAS 24: similar in scope and objectives. IAS 24 has government-related entity exemption. IAS 24 requires KMP compensation disclosure by category (US GAAP covered by SEC proxy rules for named executive officers rather than financial statements).
