# ASC-280: Segment Reporting

## What It Addresses
How a public company reports information about its operating segments and related disclosures about products/services, geographic areas, and major customers.

## Key Requirements
- Management approach: segments reflect internal reporting structure used by CODM
- Quantitative thresholds: 10% of combined revenue/profit-loss/assets
- 75% test: reportable segments must represent ≥75% of consolidated revenue
- Reconcile segment totals to consolidated financial statements
- Entity-wide disclosures: revenues from external customers by product/service; revenues and long-lived assets by geography; 10%+ customer disclosure

## Key Disclosures
- Segment revenues, profit/loss, assets
- Interest revenue and expense if reviewed by CODM
- Depreciation, depletion, amortisation per segment
- Reconciliation of segment measures to consolidated

## Key Differences from IFRS
IFRS 8 is the IFRS equivalent. Substantially converged. Both use management approach.
