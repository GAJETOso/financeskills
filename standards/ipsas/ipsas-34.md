# IPSAS-34: Separate Financial Statements

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for investments in controlled entities in separate (non-consolidated) financial statements of a public sector parent.

## Scope
PSEs presenting separate (statutory) financial statements alongside consolidated statements.

## Key Requirements
- Investments in controlled entities: cost, equity method, or IFRS 9 equivalent
- Dividends from controlled entities: P&L when right established
- Impairment indicators for investments

## Key Disclosures
- List of controlled entities: name, location, ownership %
- Measurement method for investments
- Parent's separate vs. consolidated financial statements distinction

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 27. Same principles for public sector parent entities.
