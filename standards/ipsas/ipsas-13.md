# IPSAS-13: Leases

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for leases (now superseded by IPSAS 43 which introduces single on-balance-sheet model similar to IFRS 16).

## Scope
PSEs with lease arrangements (old standard — being replaced).

## Key Requirements
- Finance lease: on-balance-sheet for lessee; operating lease: off-balance-sheet
- Superseded — see IPSAS 43 for current requirements

## Key Disclosures
- Finance lease: asset and liability recognised; operating lease: payments disclosed

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Old model analogous to IAS 17. IPSAS 43 (effective 2025) aligns with IFRS 16.
