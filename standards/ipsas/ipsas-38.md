# IPSAS-38: Disclosure of Interests in Other Entities

## What It Addresses
Requires disclosures about interests in controlled entities, associates, joint arrangements, and unconsolidated structured entities.

## Scope
All PSEs with interests in other entities.

## Key Requirements
- Significant judgements in determining control; nature of interests
- Controlled entities: NCI information; restrictions on access
- Joint ventures and associates: summarised financial information; risks
- Unconsolidated structured entities: maximum exposure to loss; financial support

## Key Disclosures
- Nature of interests and associated risks
- Structured entity disclosures: description; financial support provided

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IFRS 12. Expanded to cover public sector complex ownership arrangements.
