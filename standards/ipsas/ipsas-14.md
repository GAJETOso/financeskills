# IPSAS-14: Events After the Reporting Date

## What It Addresses
Prescribes when a PSE should adjust its financial statements for events occurring after the reporting date.

## Scope
All PSEs preparing accrual IPSAS financial statements.

## Key Requirements
- Adjusting events: evidence of conditions at reporting date — adjust statements
- Non-adjusting events: disclose only
- Date of authorisation for issue: required disclosure

## Key Disclosures
- Date statements authorised for issue
- Material non-adjusting events: nature and estimated financial effect

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 10. Essentially identical for public sector.
