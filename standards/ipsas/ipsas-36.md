# IPSAS-36: Investments in Associates and Joint Ventures

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for investments in associates and joint ventures using the equity method.

## Scope
PSEs with investments in entities where they have significant influence or joint control.

## Key Requirements
- Equity method: initial cost adjusted for share of post-acquisition surplus/deficit and OCI
- Significant influence: presumed ≥20% voting power or equivalent
- Joint venture: joint control with rights to net assets; equity method only
- Impairment: test annually or when indicators exist

## Key Disclosures
- Fair value of listed associate/JV investments
- Summarised financial information for material associates/JVs
- Unrecognised share of losses

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 28. Government may hold non-controlling stakes in private companies (state-invested enterprises) — accounted under equity method.
