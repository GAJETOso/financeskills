# IPSAS-17: Property, Plant, and Equipment

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for PP&E of PSEs including infrastructure assets, heritage assets recognition, and community assets.

## Scope
PSEs holding PP&E including government infrastructure (roads, bridges, drainage) and heritage assets.

## Key Requirements
- Recognition at cost or deemed cost on transition
- Subsequent: cost model or revaluation model (same choice as IAS 16)
- Heritage assets: encouraged but not required to be recognised; if recognised, cost or fair value model
- Infrastructure assets (roads, networks): component approach for depreciation
- Revaluation: regular revaluation to ensure carrying amount not materially different from FV

## Key Disclosures
- Carrying amounts by class
- Reconciliation of opening/closing carrying amounts
- Restrictions on title; assets pledged as security
- For revalued assets: effective date and independent valuer

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 16. Key IPSAS difference: heritage assets treatment; infrastructure asset componentisation guidance; community assets that generate no cash flows.
