# IPSAS-40: Public Sector Combinations

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for public sector combinations — amalgamations and acquisitions — including combinations under common control and restructurings of government entities.

## Scope
PSEs involved in combining, restructuring, or transferring operations between public sector entities.

## Key Requirements
- Acquisition method: applies when private sector entity acquired by government entity (similar to IFRS 3)
- Amalgamation: combination of public sector entities NOT involving acquisition — use modified pooling method (carrying amounts; no goodwill)
- Transfers of functions: assets/liabilities at carrying amounts
- Common control combinations: use amalgamation/modified pooling approach

## Key Disclosures
- Method of accounting for combination
- Date of combination and description
- Assets transferred, liabilities assumed, net assets received

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IFRS 3 but expanded for public sector-specific combinations (restructuring of government departments, nationalisation, transfer of functions).
