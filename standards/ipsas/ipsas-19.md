# IPSAS-19: Provisions, Contingent Liabilities and Contingent Assets

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for provisions and disclosures of contingent items in public sector, including government guarantees and litigation.

## Scope
All PSEs.

## Key Requirements
- Provision: present obligation; probable outflow; reliable estimate
- Government guarantees: assess probability of call; recognise provision when probable
- Public sector specific: provision for legal claims against government; tax refund obligations; decommissioning of government assets
- Restructuring of government agencies: recognise provision when formal plan communicated
- Contingent liabilities: disclose unless remote
- Contingent assets: disclose when probable; recognise when virtually certain

## Key Disclosures
- Provisions by class: opening/closing, additions, used, reversed, unwinding
- For contingent liabilities: description, estimated financial effect, uncertainties

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 37. Virtually identical principles applied to public sector context.
