# IPSAS-46: Measurement

## What It Addresses
Provides a single source of measurement guidance for IPSAS, adapting IFRS 13 fair value framework for public sector including service potential and cost of replacement.

## Scope
All PSEs measuring assets and liabilities.

## Key Requirements
- Measurement bases: historical cost, current value (fair value, value in use, fulfilment value), cost of replacement
- Fair value for cash-generating assets: exit price (aligned with IFRS 13 hierarchy)
- Replacement cost for non-cash-generating assets: cost to replace the service potential
- Fulfilment value for liabilities: cost to fulfil obligation
- Hierarchy for non-financial assets: Level 1 (market), Level 2 (observable), Level 3 (unobservable/cost approach)

## Key Disclosures
- Measurement basis used for significant assets/liabilities
- Level 3 measurements: techniques, inputs, sensitivity
- Replacement cost for public infrastructure

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Based on IFRS 13 but adapted: replacement cost (depreciated reproduction/replacement cost) is the primary measurement basis for non-cash-generating public assets instead of exit price.
