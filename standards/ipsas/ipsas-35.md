# IPSAS-35: Consolidated Financial Statements

## What It Addresses
Establishes principles for consolidated financial statements of public sector groups, including criteria for control.

## Scope
PSEs that control one or more other entities (whole-of-government, ministry groups, government departments with agencies).

## Key Requirements
- Control: power + exposure to variable returns + ability to use power to affect returns
- Whole-of-government: consolidate all controlled public sector entities (GGS + public corporations)
- Investment entity exception for government venture funds
- Uniform accounting policies; alignment of reporting dates
- Eliminate intercompany balances and transactions; unrealised surpluses/deficits

## Key Disclosures
- Composition of consolidated group
- Nature and extent of restrictions on access to net assets of controlled entities
- Significant judgements in determining control

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IFRS 10. Expanded to cover whole-of-government consolidation including government departments, agencies, and commercial entities.
