# IPSAS-29: Financial Instruments: Recognition and Measurement

## What It Addresses
Prescribes recognition and measurement principles for financial instruments held by PSEs. Being superseded by IPSAS 41.

## Scope
PSEs with financial assets and liabilities.

## Key Requirements
- Four categories: fair value through surplus/deficit (FVSD); held-to-maturity; loans and receivables; available-for-sale
- Impairment: incurred loss model (being replaced by expected loss model in IPSAS 41)
- Hedge accounting: fair value, cash flow, net investment hedges

## Key Disclosures
- Carrying amounts by category
- Gains/losses by category

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 39. Now superseded by IPSAS 41 (adapted from IFRS 9).
