# IPSAS-44: Non-current Assets Held for Sale and Discontinued Operations

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for PSE assets classified as held for sale and discontinued operations (e.g., privatisation of government enterprises).

## Scope
PSEs disposing of assets or operations through sale or transfer.

## Key Requirements
- Held for sale: available for immediate sale in present condition + sale highly probable
- Measure at lower of carrying amount and FV less costs to sell; no depreciation
- Discontinued operations: separate major line of public service or geographical area
- Privatisations: government selling state-owned enterprise — discontinued operation treatment if qualifies

## Key Disclosures
- Assets held for sale: separate balance sheet line
- Discontinued operations: surplus/deficit on separate P&L line
- Plans for disposal; timing; method (sale, transfer, privatisation)

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IFRS 5. Public sector context: privatisation of state enterprises is the primary trigger for discontinued operations.
