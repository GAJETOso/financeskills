# IPSAS-27: Agriculture

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting treatment for biological assets and agricultural produce of PSEs (e.g., government forestry, fisheries, state farms).

## Scope
PSEs with agricultural activities managed by government agencies.

## Key Requirements
- Biological assets: measure at FV less costs to sell
- FV changes: recognised in surplus/deficit
- Agricultural produce at harvest: FV less costs to sell becomes cost for subsequent accounting
- Government grants for biological assets at FVLCTS: recognise when receivable (unconditional) or when conditions met (conditional)

## Key Disclosures
- Aggregate gain/loss from FV changes
- Description of biological assets; FV measurement methodology
- Biological assets pledged as security

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 41. Same fair value model applied to government agricultural activities.
