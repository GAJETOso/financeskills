# IPSAS-11: Construction Contracts

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for construction contracts in public sector, including government infrastructure projects.

## Scope
PSEs undertaking construction contracts as contractors (e.g., government construction agencies).

## Key Requirements
- Percentage of completion method (stage of completion)
- Contract costs: direct + attributable indirect costs + costs specifically chargeable to customer
- Contract revenue recognised proportional to stage of completion when outcome can be estimated reliably
- When outcome cannot be estimated reliably: recognise revenue only to extent of recoverable costs incurred
- Expected losses: recognised immediately

## Key Disclosures
- Revenue and costs recognised from construction contracts
- Methods for determining stage of completion
- For contracts in progress: gross amounts due from/to customers

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 11. Now largely superseded by IFRS 15 in private sector; public sector retains IPSAS 11 for government construction activities.
