# IPSAS-20: Related Party Disclosures

## What It Addresses
Requires PSEs to disclose related party relationships and transactions including government ministers, government-owned entities, and key management.

## Scope
All PSEs.

## Key Requirements
- Related parties: persons with control/significant influence; entities under common control; key management personnel (KMP) of entity or government
- Government-related entities: transactions with entities controlled by same government may be individually or collectively disclosed
- KMP compensation: total and by category
- Transactions with related parties: amount, outstanding balances, terms

## Key Disclosures
- KMP compensation by category
- Related party transactions: description, amount, outstanding balances
- Government-related entity disclosures

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 24. Key addition: government-related entity concept broader in public sector (ministers, government departments).
