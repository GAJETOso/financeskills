# IPSAS-22: Disclosure of Financial Information about the General Government Sector

## What It Addresses
Requires governments that publish whole-of-government financial statements to disclose information about the general government sector (GGS) — the core government budget sector.

## Scope
Governments (national/sub-national) that present whole-of-government financial statements including public corporations.

## Key Requirements
- GGS defined per IMF Government Finance Statistics (GFS) framework
- Disclose GGS: statement of financial position, financial performance, cash flows on same basis as whole-of-government
- Reconcile GGS to whole-of-government statements
- GGS excludes public financial and non-financial corporations

## Key Disclosures
- GGS financial statements with reconciliation to whole-of-government
- Basis for distinguishing GGS from public corporations

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
No direct private sector equivalent. Related to government statistical reporting (GFS/ESA).
