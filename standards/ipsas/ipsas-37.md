# IPSAS-37: Joint Arrangements

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for joint arrangements: joint operations (line-by-line) and joint ventures (equity method).

## Scope
PSEs party to joint arrangements (government joint infrastructure projects, joint venture state enterprises).

## Key Requirements
- Joint control: unanimous consent requirement
- Joint operation: recognise assets, liabilities, revenues, expenses proportionately
- Joint venture: equity method; proportionate consolidation no longer permitted

## Key Disclosures
- Name; nature of activity; principal place of business
- Proportion of ownership; method of accounting

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IFRS 11. Government joint infrastructure projects (cross-border bridges, shared utilities) may constitute joint operations.
