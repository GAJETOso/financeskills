# IPSAS-18: Segment Reporting

## What It Addresses
Requires PSEs to present financial information by service segments (operating segments in public sector context) or geographical segments.

## Scope
PSEs that publish financial statements with segments (particularly large government departments).

## Key Requirements
- Service segment: distinct component providing related outputs/outcomes
- Segment revenue, expenses, assets, liabilities reported separately
- Reconciliation to consolidated financial statements
- Primary and secondary segment formats

## Key Disclosures
- Identification of reportable segments; basis for identification
- Segment revenue, surplus/deficit, assets, liabilities
- Reconciliation to total entity financial statements

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 14 (predecessor to IFRS 8). Less 'management approach' orientation; more prescriptive about service vs. geographical segments in public sector.
