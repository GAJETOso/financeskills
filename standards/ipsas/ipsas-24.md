# IPSAS-24: Presentation of Budget Information in Financial Statements

## What It Addresses
Requires PSEs to provide disclosure comparing budget and actual amounts when they make their approved budget publicly available.

## Scope
PSEs that make their approved budget publicly available and are required to account for execution of that budget.

## Key Requirements
- Budget vs. actual comparison: on face of financial statements OR in notes
- Recurrent budget AND capital budget if separately approved
- Explain material differences between budget and actual
- Reconcile: budget basis to accrual basis if different (e.g., cash budget vs. accrual statements)
- Final budget: appropriations after all authorised amendments

## Key Disclosures
- Comparison of final budget and actual amounts for each major line item
- Explanation of material differences
- Reconciliation of budget basis to IPSAS accrual basis

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
No direct private sector equivalent. Unique to public sector — accountability for budget appropriations is central to government reporting.
