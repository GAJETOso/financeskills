# IPSAS-1: Presentation of Financial Statements

## What It Addresses
Prescribes the basis for presenting general purpose financial statements of public sector entities (PSEs) to ensure comparability. Adapted from IAS 1.

## Scope
PSEs including national/local governments, government agencies, and international organisations.

## Key Requirements
- Fair presentation and compliance with IPSAS
- Going concern assessment for public sector context
- Accrual basis of accounting
- Minimum components: statement of financial position, statement of financial performance, statement of changes in net assets/equity, cash flow statement, notes
- Comparative information required

## Key Disclosures
- Going concern disclosures for government entities
- Accounting policies — basis of preparation
- Judgements and estimation uncertainties

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
IAS 1 (private sector). Key difference: public sector uses 'net assets/equity' not 'equity'; revenue often non-exchange (taxes, transfers) rather than customer contracts.
