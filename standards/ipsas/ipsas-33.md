# IPSAS-33: First-time Adoption of Accrual Basis IPSAS

## What It Addresses
Provides guidance for PSEs transitioning from cash basis to accrual basis IPSAS, including transitional reliefs and exemptions.

## Scope
PSEs adopting accrual basis IPSAS for the first time (transitioning from cash basis or other basis).

## Key Requirements
- 3-year transition period: progressive adoption of recognition, measurement, and disclosure requirements
- Transitional reliefs: PP&E (deemed cost option); government securities; pension obligations; heritage assets
- First IPSAS financial statements: opening balance sheet at transition date
- Comparative information: encouraged but not required in transition period

## Key Disclosures
- Statement that this is first IPSAS financial statements
- Transitional reliefs applied and effect on financial statements
- Reconciliation from previous basis to IPSAS

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IFRS 1. Recognises that many governments starting from cash accounting need extended relief period.
