# IPSAS-43: Leases

## What It Addresses
Replaces IPSAS 13. Introduces single on-balance-sheet model for lessees, aligned with IFRS 16, adapted for public sector context.

## Scope
All PSEs with lease arrangements.

## Key Requirements
- Lessee: recognise ROU asset and lease liability for all leases
- Short-term (≤12 months) and low-value asset exemptions available
- Public sector specific: peppercorn/below-market leases with government entities — measurement at cost vs. fair value option
- Lessor: retain operating/finance lease classification
- Service concession arrangements: governed by IPSAS 32, not IPSAS 43

## Key Disclosures
- ROU assets by class
- Lease liabilities maturity analysis
- Short-term and low-value lease expense
- Below-market lease terms disclosed

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Based on IFRS 16. Key IPSAS addition: peppercorn leases (common in public sector — government leases assets to/from charities or related entities at nominal rent) at cost or fair value.
