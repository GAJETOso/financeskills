# IPSAS-10: Financial Reporting in Hyperinflationary Economies

## What It Addresses
Prescribes restatement of financial statements of PSEs in hyperinflationary economies using a general price index.

## Scope
PSEs in economies with hyperinflation (using same indicators as IAS 29).

## Key Requirements
- Restate non-monetary items using general price index from date of acquisition
- Monetary items: not restated
- Gain/loss on net monetary position: recognised in surplus/deficit
- All comparative figures restated to current-period measuring unit

## Key Disclosures
- Fact of restatement; price index used; gain/loss on monetary position

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 29. Same principles applied to government and public sector entities.
