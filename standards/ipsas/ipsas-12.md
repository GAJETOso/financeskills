# IPSAS-12: Inventories

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for inventories held by public sector entities including goods for distribution, military supplies, and work in progress.

## Scope
PSEs holding inventories: goods for distribution at nil/nominal charge, work in progress, consumable stores.

## Key Requirements
- Measure at lower of cost and net realisable value (or replacement cost for inventories held for distribution at no/nominal charge)
- FIFO or weighted average cost formula (no LIFO)
- Net realisable value: estimated selling price less costs to complete and sell
- Replacement cost used for public goods distributed free: carrying amount compared to current replacement cost

## Key Disclosures
- Accounting policy (cost formula)
- Total carrying amount and carrying amount by category
- Amount of inventories recognised as expense or write-down

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 2. Key difference: replacement cost measurement for public goods distributed at no/nominal charge.
