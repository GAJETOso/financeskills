# IPSAS-45: Property, Plant, and Equipment

## What It Addresses
Replaces IPSAS 17 for applicable entities. Updated PP&E standard with enhanced measurement guidance aligned with IFRS updates.

## Scope
PSEs holding PP&E.

## Key Requirements
- Cost model or revaluation model
- Component approach: depreciate significant components separately
- Heritage assets: may recognise at cost or FV; specific heritage asset disclosures
- Infrastructure assets: specialist valuation approaches
- Useful life reviews at minimum each reporting date
- Derecognition: gain/loss on disposal to P&L

## Key Disclosures
- PP&E by class: carrying amount, depreciation method, useful lives
- Heritage assets recognised and not recognised
- Infrastructure asset condition assessments and renewal expenditure

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Updated from IPSAS 17 (adapted from IAS 16). Enhanced guidance on heritage assets and infrastructure — unique public sector asset classes.
