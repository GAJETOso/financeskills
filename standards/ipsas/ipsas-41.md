# IPSAS-41: Financial Instruments

## What It Addresses
Replaces IPSAS 29. Prescribes recognition and measurement of financial instruments for PSEs based on the IFRS 9 model including ECL impairment.

## Scope
All PSEs with financial assets and liabilities.

## Key Requirements
- Classification based on business model and SPPI test (adapted for public sector)
- Three categories: amortised cost; FVOCI; FVTPL
- ECL model: 3-stage for public sector lending (sovereign loans, development finance)
- Hedge accounting: objectives-based effectiveness test
- Public sector specifics: government loan forgiveness; concessional loans (below-market rates — day 1 loss recognised)

## Key Disclosures
- Financial assets by classification
- ECL allowance rollforward
- Hedge accounting objectives and effects
- Concessional loans: fair value at inception and carrying amounts

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Based on IFRS 9. Key public sector additions: concessional (below-market) loans are common in development finance; day-1 difference between face value and fair value recognised as grant expense.
