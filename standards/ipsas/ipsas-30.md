# IPSAS-30: Financial Instruments: Disclosures

## What It Addresses
Prescribes disclosures for PSEs about financial instruments, including credit risk, liquidity risk, and market risk.

## Scope
All PSEs holding financial instruments.

## Key Requirements
- Significance of financial instruments to financial position and performance
- Nature and extent of risks: credit, liquidity, market
- Government-specific: sovereign risk; foreign exchange reserve management; central bank lending facilities
- Quantitative sensitivity analysis for market risks

## Key Disclosures
- Financial instruments by category; fair value; collateral
- Credit risk: maximum exposure; quality; concentrations; sovereign exposure
- Liquidity risk: maturity analysis of financial liabilities
- Market risk: sensitivity analysis (interest rate, currency, price)

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IFRS 7. Added public sector context: sovereign lending, development finance, central bank instruments.
