# IPSAS-28: Financial Instruments: Presentation

## What It Addresses
Prescribes principles for presenting financial instruments of PSEs as liabilities or equity, and for offsetting.

## Scope
PSEs holding financial instruments.

## Key Requirements
- Equity vs. liability: obligation test
- Government debt instruments: classify as liabilities
- Central bank instruments (currency): special consideration
- Compound instruments: split liability and equity components

## Key Disclosures
- Financial instruments by category
- Compound instrument components

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 32. Applied to government financial instruments, central bank notes, Treasury instruments.
