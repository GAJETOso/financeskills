# IPSAS-5: Borrowing Costs

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for borrowing costs; allows but does not require capitalisation of qualifying asset borrowing costs.

## Scope
All PSEs with borrowing costs.

## Key Requirements
- Benchmark treatment: expense all borrowing costs as incurred
- Allowed alternative: capitalise directly attributable costs for qualifying assets
- Government entities often use benchmark treatment for consistency and transparency
- Qualifying asset: substantial period to prepare for intended use

## Key Disclosures
- Borrowing costs capitalised during period
- Capitalisation rate applied

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 23. IPSAS 5 retains the option to expense (benchmark) which IAS 23 eliminated — recognising that many governments expense borrowing costs.
