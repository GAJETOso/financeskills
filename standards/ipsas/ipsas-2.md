# IPSAS-2: Cash Flow Statements

## What It Addresses
Requires information about historical changes in cash and cash equivalents of public sector entities via cash flow statement.

## Scope
All PSEs preparing accrual basis IPSAS financial statements.

## Key Requirements
- Direct or indirect method for operating activities
- Investing activities: capital expenditure; acquisition/disposal of investments
- Financing activities: borrowings; principal repayments; equity transactions with government owner
- Third-party payments on behalf of entity
- Receipts from taxation and grants classified as operating or investing based on activity

## Key Disclosures
- Components of cash and cash equivalents
- Restricted cash balances of government entities
- Material non-cash investing/financing transactions

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
IAS 7 (private sector). Public sector has additional complexity: grants, tax receipts, third-party payment flows.
