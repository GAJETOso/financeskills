# IPSAS-31: Intangible Assets

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for intangible assets of PSEs including government-held intellectual property, licences, and heritage intellectual assets.

## Scope
PSEs holding intangible assets.

## Key Requirements
- Recognition: identifiable; controlled; future economic benefits or service potential
- Internally generated intangibles: R&D phase distinction
- Heritage intellectual assets (e.g., archives, government databases): encouraged recognition; FV or historical cost
- Finite/indefinite useful life classification; amortisation or impairment only

## Key Disclosures
- Gross carrying amount and accumulated amortisation by class
- R&D costs recognised as expense during period
- Intangible assets with indefinite useful life and reason

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 38. Key addition: heritage intellectual assets; government databases; creative works (public archives).
