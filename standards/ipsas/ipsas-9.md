# IPSAS-9: Revenue from Exchange Transactions

## What It Addresses
Prescribes accounting for revenue from exchange transactions (where entity receives assets of approximately equal value) in public sector.

## Scope
PSEs generating revenues from exchange transactions (fees for services, sale of goods, interest, royalties, dividends).

## Key Requirements
- Revenue recognition: when probable that future economic benefits will flow to entity AND benefits can be measured reliably
- Sale of goods: transfer of significant risks and rewards
- Rendering of services: stage of completion method
- Interest, royalties, dividends: specific recognition criteria

## Key Disclosures
- Accounting policy for revenue recognition
- Amount of revenue by significant category
- Amount of revenue from exchange transactions with related parties

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 18 (predecessor to IFRS 15). IPSAS has not yet fully converged with IFRS 15's 5-step model for public sector exchange transactions.
