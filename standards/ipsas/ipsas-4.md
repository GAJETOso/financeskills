# IPSAS-4: The Effects of Changes in Foreign Exchange Rates

## What It Addresses
Prescribes how to include foreign currency transactions and translate foreign operations' financial statements for PSEs.

## Scope
PSEs with foreign currency transactions or foreign operations.

## Key Requirements
- Functional currency determination
- Foreign currency transactions: record at spot rate; monetary items at closing rate
- Translation differences: P&L for monetary items; OCI for foreign operations
- Government foreign currency reserves: typically monetary assets retranslated at closing rate

## Key Disclosures
- Exchange differences in P&L
- Translation adjustments in OCI and reconciliation

## Relationship to Private Sector Standard
Adapted from IAS 21. Public sector specific: treatment of government foreign exchange reserves and IMF Special Drawing Rights (SDRs).
