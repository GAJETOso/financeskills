# Coverage Ratios

Measure capacity to service debt and other fixed financial obligations.

## Interest Coverage Ratio (ICR)
**Formula:** EBIT ÷ Interest Expense  
**Interpretation:** Higher = more comfortable debt servicing. < 1.5 = stressed; 2.0–3.0 = minimum for investment grade; > 5.0 = strong.  
**IFRS Note:** Post-IFRS 16, lease interest is included in interest expense — ICR typically lower vs. pre-IFRS 16.  
**US GAAP Note:** ASC 842 operating leases kept as operating expense — lease interest not in denominator; ICR higher.

## EBITDA Interest Coverage
**Formula:** EBITDA ÷ Interest Expense  
**Interpretation:** More conservative denominator than EBIT; preferred in credit covenants. Lease-adjusted EBITDA commonly used.

## Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR)
**Formula:** Net Operating Income ÷ Total Debt Service  
**Where Total Debt Service =** Principal Repayment + Interest Expense  
**Interpretation:** > 1.25 generally required by lenders; < 1.0 = cannot cover debt service from operations.  
**Use:** Project finance; real estate; infrastructure — standard covenant metric.

## Fixed Charge Coverage Ratio (FCCR)
**Formula:** (EBIT + Fixed Charges) ÷ (Fixed Charges + Interest)  
**Where Fixed Charges =** Lease payments, preferred dividends, etc.  
**Interpretation:** Broader than ICR; captures all fixed financial obligations. > 1.5 comfortable.

## Cash Coverage Ratio
**Formula:** (EBIT + Depreciation & Amortisation) ÷ Interest Expense  
**Interpretation:** Approximates cash available to cover interest (EBITDA-based but without working capital adjustments). More reliable than accrual-based ICR.

## Asset Coverage Ratio
**Formula:** (Total Assets − Intangible Assets − Current Liabilities) ÷ Total Debt  
**Interpretation:** > 1.5 for senior secured debt; > 1.0 minimum. Indicates asset backing for outstanding debt.

## Preferred Dividend Coverage
**Formula:** Net Income ÷ Preferred Dividends  
**Interpretation:** > 3.0 comfortable; < 1.5 risk of dividend suspension.

## Common Judgment Areas
- IFRS 16 lease liability treatment: include/exclude lease interest and principal in debt service for DSCR
- Covenant definitions in loan agreements often specify "frozen GAAP" or specify adjustments — always use lender-defined formulas
- Non-recurring items: covenant calculations may exclude restructuring charges, impairments — verify covenant definitions
- Capitalised interest (IAS 23/ASC 835): adds to asset cost but reduces interest expense → inflates ICR slightly
